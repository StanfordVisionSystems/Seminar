About
We are a group of computer vision and systems researchers at Stanford University whose focus is on building next generation systems for video/image processing. Unlike typical machine learning or computer systems groups, our work crosses the boundary between theory and implementation. This trait has enabled us to produce useful systems that are orders of magnitude higher performance than the state of the art.
